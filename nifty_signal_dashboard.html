How to use this
Signals update every 15 min. The candle direction banner tells you whether the current 15-min candle is predicted to close UP, DOWN, or SIDEWAYS vs the open. Confidence >65% = stronger lean. Always enter on confirmation — a follow-through candle, not just the signal. Use ATM options. Set SL at the prior candle's high/low. VIX >20 = reduce position size by 30–50%.
